Get Involved

Tharollo NPO Foundation believes that change begins with people who are willing to act. There are many ways for you to be part of our mission and help us bring solutions to those in need. Whether you give your time, your resources, or your skills, your involvement makes a meaningful difference. Volunteers help us deliver food parcels, organize educational workshops, and run wellness programs that touch lives daily.

Donors and partners sustain our work by ensuring that we have the resources needed to keep supporting families. Every contribution, no matter how small, creates ripples of hope and progress. Getting involved with Tharollo means joining a family that cares, acts, and believes in a brighter future for all.
