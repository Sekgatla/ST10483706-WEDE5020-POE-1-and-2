About Tharollo NPO

Tharollo NPO Foundation is dedicated to community empowerment and support programs that improve lives.

We believe that by working together with schools, organisations, and local leaders, we can address challenges such as poverty, lack of education, and limited access to wellness resources. Our work focuses on bringing hope, dignity, and opportunity to those who need it most.
